# Setup a Managed Node

A managed Blacklight node lets you participate in Blacklight without running any infrastructure yourself. The node is run on your behalf - you simply stake your NIL tokens and start earning rewards.

The minimum stake for a managed node is 500,000 NIL. You can unstake at any point, subject to a 7 day unbonding period. When claiming rewards, a 30% fee applies if you withdraw rewards to your wallet, or a 20% fee if you restake them directly.

## Prerequisites

Ensure you have NIL and ETH on Ethereum (L1) before getting started.

To obtain NIL, you can either bridge your Cosmos NIL
([see here for details](/blacklight/tools/bridging#from-cosmos-to-ethereum)) or buy or swap to NIL
on a decentralised exchange, for example Uniswap.

## Walkthrough

Below is a step-by-step walkthrough of the full process of setting up your managed node using the [webapp](https://managed-blacklight.nillion.com/). If you get stuck, feel free to reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/nillionnetwork).

### Bridge NIL to Nillion's L2

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '2rem', alignItems: 'flex-start', marginBottom: '1rem', flexWrap: 'wrap'}}>
  <div style={{flex: '1', minWidth: '300px'}}>
    <p>First, bridge your NIL tokens from Ethereum to Nillion's L2. The webapp will guide you through this process.</p>

    1. Connect your wallet
    2. Click "Bridge NIL" and you will be taken to the bridge and connect your wallet there
    3. Ensure `Ethereum` is the source chain and `Nillion` is the destination
    4. Select `NIL` as the token to bridge
    5. Enter the amount of `NIL` you want to bridge (at least 500,000 NIL to meet the minimum stake)
    6. Review and confirm the transaction

    <p>Your NIL tokens will be available on Nillion's L2 after the bridge transaction is confirmed.</p>

### Bridge ETH to Nillion's L2

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '2rem', alignItems: 'flex-start', marginBottom: '1rem', flexWrap: 'wrap'}}>
  <div style={{flex: '1', minWidth: '300px'}}>
    <p>Next, bridge ETH from Ethereum to Nillion's L2. This ETH is used to cover gas fees on Nillion's L2.</p>

    1. Click "Bridge ETH" and you will be taken to the bridge and connect your wallet there
    2. Ensure `Ethereum` is the source chain and `Nillion` is the destination
    3. Select `ETH` as the token to bridge
    4. Enter the amount of `ETH` you want to bridge (this is to cover gas, we recommend at least 0.001)
    5. Review and confirm the transaction

    <p>Once the bridge transaction is confirmed, your ETH will be available on Nillion's L2.</p>
