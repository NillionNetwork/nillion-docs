# Faucets

Faucets allow you to obtain test tokens for use on Blacklight Testnet. This guide explains how to get ETH and NIL tokens for testing purposes.

## Get ETH on Blacklight Testnet

To get ETH on Blacklight Testnet, you'll need to first obtain Sepolia ETH and then bridge it to Blacklight Testnet.

### Step 1: Get Sepolia ETH

1. Visit a Sepolia faucet, such as the [Conduit Faucet](https://faucet.conduit.xyz/)
2. Connect your wallet and request Sepolia ETH
3. Wait for the transaction to complete

### Step 2: Bridge to Blacklight Testnet

1. Visit the [Blacklight Testnet Bridge](https://nilav-shzvox09l5-8e94b8f60d47a72f.testnets.rollbridge.app/) to bridge from Sepolia to Blacklight Testnet
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select Sepolia as the source chain and Blacklight Testnet as the destination
4. Enter the amount of ETH you want to bridge
5. Complete the bridge transaction

Once the bridge transaction is confirmed, your ETH will be available on Blacklight Testnet.

## Get NIL on Blacklight Testnet

A direct NIL faucet for Blacklight Testnet is not yet available.
